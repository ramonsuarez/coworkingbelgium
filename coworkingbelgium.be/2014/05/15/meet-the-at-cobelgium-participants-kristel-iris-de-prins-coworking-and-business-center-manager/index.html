Meet the @cobelgium participants: Kristel Iris De Prins, coworking and business center manager

15/05/2014 ramonsuarez

Kristel runs VIPOFFICES, an hybrid center with the services of Business centers, virtual offices and the possibility to choose a coworking formula.

She believes a lot in the value of coworking: she thinks that coworking makes life human,
empathic,
dynamic and above all real.

You can contact Kristel on Twitter,  LinkedIn or Facebook.
